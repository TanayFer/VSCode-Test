Vision Mission Location

Vision

To cultivate critical thinkers, problem solvers, and ethical professionals through education, driving regional & national development.

Mission

  • Promote research and innovation that address real world challenges, focusing on sustainability, ecology & conservation
  • Integrate advanced technologies into all aspects of education and research
  • Deliver academic excellence by equipping students with knowledge, skills & ethical mindset needed for success
  • To form an inclusive community that encourages interdisciplinary collaboration and intellectual curiosity
  • Inspire a commitment to lifelong learning, preparing students to contribute to regional, national & global development in alignment with the school's vision

Our Location

The Deccan Education Society's Pune University is located in Pune, Maharashtra, India. The campus is situated in a serene environment, surrounded by lush greenery and scenic views. It is easily accessible by road and public transport, making it convenient for students and faculty to commute.
