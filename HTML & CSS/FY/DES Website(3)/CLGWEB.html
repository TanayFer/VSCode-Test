Deccan Education Society - Legacy of 140 Years

Our Story

Welcome to the Deccan Education Society (DES) - a distinguished institution with a legacy spanning 140 years. DES holds a unique place in the history of modern India, marking the beginning of a new era since its establishment in 1884.

Guided by an unwavering commitment to high-quality education, DES has fostered intellectual growth and holistic development. Founders like Lokmanya Bal Gangadhar Tilak and Gopal Ganesh Agarkar envisioned an education system to empower individuals and shape the nation.

Welcome to DES PU

DES Pune University (DES PU) is a dynamic institution poised to redefine higher education. Rooted in the legacy of DES's excellence, DES PU is committed to research, creativity, and innovation.
